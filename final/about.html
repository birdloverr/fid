Our Origin Story

It all started back in 1992 - our founder, Linda Gray, loved flowers and was looking for community in the bay area. With the support of a small group of her co-workers, she started the Sakura Floral Society.
Her and her friends from work would put up flyers advertising the society. At the time, they met in a local library. Now more than 30 years later, those humble beginnings have led to multiple locations all across northern California (with more in the works) and a community of over 400 floral-loving friends.
